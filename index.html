Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

TIC VTU AND FASHION ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our application.

1. Data Collection

We may collect the following information when you use our app:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Transaction and wallet data (if applicable)

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process transactions and wallet activities
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve app services

3. Account Deletion (Data Removal Request)

You have the right to request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

How to Request Deletion

You can request account deletion using either of the following methods:

  • Send an email to: support@ticvtuandfashion.com
  • Or use the "Delete Account" option inside the app (if available)

Email Format Example

Subject: Account Deletion Request
Message: Please delete my TIC VTU AND FASHION account and all associated data registered with this email/phone number: [your email or phone number]

What Happens After Request

  • Your account will be deactivated immediately upon verification
  • All personal data (profile, wallet, transaction history) will be deleted within 30 days
  • Some data may be retained if required by law (e.g., fraud prevention or financial records)

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.
